*Databases and SQL*
What is database normalization?
-Taking recursive data and splitting into a normal table.

What are joins?
-Command used in sequel to join multiple tables together, pets and owners, see which pets does sarah own.

What are junction tables?
-Junction tables are tables that contain the primary keys of the two tables you want to relate. only used for many to many.

What is a query?
-Select, get data out of database. A request to obtain data.

What are the keywords to find, remove, and change data?
-select, delete, update
